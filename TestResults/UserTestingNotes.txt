Dawgs Marketplace - User Testing Notes
=======================================================================

PARTICIPANT 1
Date: March 13, 2026

- Liked that the marketplace is restricted to UW students only
- Said knowing the buyer has a UW email makes them feel trustworthy
- Photos are important — needs to see item condition before buying
- Descriptions should be simple with price and item name easy to spot
- A button to email the seller directly is enough, no need for built-in chat
- Seller reviews are not necessary since UW verification already builds trust
- Would like a way to search by course or subject for textbooks


PARTICIPANT 2
Date: March 13, 2026

- The UW-only restriction was the most appealing part of the app
- Felt safer meeting a fellow student than a stranger from a general website
- Clear photos showing item condition are the most important part of a listing
- The search bar was the one feature they said the site must have
- Email contact is enough - already checks school email throughout the day
- Seller ratings could be a nice addition but are not essential


KEY TAKEAWAYS
- Both users responded positively to the UW-only, verified-student concept
- The @uw.edu login requirement directly addressed trust and safety concerns
- Search and clear photos were the most requested features
- Simple email contact was preferred over a built-in messaging system
